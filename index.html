About us

We are a startup studio. We work as an active co-founder on every startup providing expertise, network and funding. We have a clear focus on these industries: Internet, Events, Music and Services, helping us to give the best value to our partners.
We are builders.


What is a Startup Studio?

A startup studio is a company that creates startups with shared resources like office, mentors and funding. We are a team of entrepreneurs, developers and designers who have the necessary experience to build a solid business with the right direction.
