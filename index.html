About us

We are a startup studio. We work as an active co-founder on every startup providing expertise, network and funding. We have a clear focus on this industries: Internet, Events, Music and Services (SMB). This helps us to give the best value to our partners and help them to reach the next level.


What is a Startup Studio?

A startup studio it's a company that creates startups with shared resources like office, team, mentors and funding. We are a team of entrepreneurs who have the necessary experience to build a solid business with the right direction. We also have a group of mentors who help us constantly improve the performance of our businesses.
